Introduction

Welcome to our esteemed institution!

We are a renowned academy dedicated to empowering individuals who aspire to achieve their goals and make a positive impact on our beloved nation. With our top-notch coaching and unwavering commitment to excellence, we strive to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Impact Academy is a visionary institution that aims to revolutionize the landscape of education. Founded by passionate and driven engineers, we have embarked on a mission to disrupt traditional learning methods. Our approach is rooted in making learning an enjoyable and engaging experience, fostering creativity and innovation in every aspect of education. We are committed to empowering students with the latest knowledge and skills, propelling them towards a future filled with endless possibilities. Join us on this exhilarating journey as we pave the way for a new era of education!

Our Vision

Design and develop an eco-system for all student community to transform their ideation to innovation. We want to help them to unlock their ability to think out of box to bring out incredible creations

Our Mission

Promote the spirit of Science and technology among young minds to help them become innovators. We believe a collective effort of parents, teachers and mentors to nurture sprouting ideas of a kid will create favorable impact.

We believe in connecting ignited minds in a matrix to bring in collaborative learning which will drive them towards excellence. We at Impact Academy provide fun-filled learning of science and technology. We provide a platform for the students to explore their talents and come up with crazy ideas.

Our team has been doing research on learning ability of students of different age group and innovative learning tools are being developed. We have engaged over 1000 rural students in our scholar program named SPARK or you can spell it as Science Park in association with Sarva Shikashana Abiyana- Karnataka.

We are also catering engineering students developing their skill to get an employment in suitable industry. We are also focused of strengthening the roots of budding engineers by providing them industrial Training program and make them understand needs of the Industry.
